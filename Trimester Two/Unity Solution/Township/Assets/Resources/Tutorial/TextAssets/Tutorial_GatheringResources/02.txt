Every job in Township requires a citizen of a specific profession to work it.

Workmen will work all labour-based jobs, such as hauling resources, building and using the marketcart.

Lumberjacks will work in a Lumberjack's Hut and provide Wood to the town.

Quarrymen will mine a Quarry and provide Brick to the town.

You can see what type of profession is required for a job by opening the Jobs Panel.
You can also see which profession is required for a building by clicking on the building.
