Merchants will arrive from time to time, and propose a set of deals.

Each Merchant will have their own preference of Resources that they wish to buy or sell.

Merchants may propose one deal, or many. Each deal can contain multiple items and the direction of trade can change within deals.

Be sure to read the deals carefully, as Merchants wont reverse a deal that has been accepted.

Look out for the best price, as each Merchant will buy or sell Resources at their own rates.

You wont be able to accept any deals that you can't afford.
